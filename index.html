Quick Amenities

Specialised Therapy

Some mental health issues necessitate a highly specific form of treatment or therapy under serious conditions.

Couple Therapy

Couples therapy is a type of psychotherapy that can help you and your partner better understand and manage your relationship. Couples counselling can benefit any couple, regardless of their sexual orientation or age.

Children Counselling

Child counselling is a therapy aimed at young children, teenagers, and adolescents who are suffering from emotional and mental distress.

Parental Counselling

Parent counselling is a nonjudgmental service that aims to give you information, advice, resources, and emotional support. It focuses on how you, as a parent, influence your family's dynamics.

Corporate Counselling

Corporate counselling tries to reduce employee stress levels and assist them in choosing healthier lifestyle choices.

School / Colleges Counselling

School counseling is provided to students in grades K-12 in both public and private schools. Counseling is intended to help kids achieve academic success, improve their behaviour and attendance, and develop socially.
